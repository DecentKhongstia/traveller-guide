About Us

Welcome to Ri Khasi Trails

"Ri" means Land , a nod to khasi land and traditions "

Where Memories Last Forever

At our core, we don’t just offer trips — We offer stories, feelings, and unforgettable moments.

In the heart of Meghalaya, among the lush hills and flowing rivers, lies a world where time slows down and simplicity takes over.
We invite you to live like the Khasi people, experience their traditions, walk their paths, and share in their everyday joys.

💚 We don’t chase profit — we pursue purpose.
Because to us, true hospitality isn’t just about services.
It’s about real, human connections.
It’s about genuine smiles, shared meals, and moments that stay with you forever.

Let us take you beyond the usual.
Live the Khasi way.
Come as a guest, leave as family.
